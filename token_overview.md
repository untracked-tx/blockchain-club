### Key

- **Soulbound:** Cannot be transferred once minted.
- **Mint Access:**  
  - `OFFICER_ONLY`: Only officers can mint  
  - `WHITELIST_ONLY`: Only whitelisted users can mint  
  - `PUBLIC`: Anyone can mint
